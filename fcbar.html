About FC Barcelona

FC Barcelona, founded in 1899, is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the world. Known for its rich history, commitment to beautiful football, and legendary players, Barça is more than a club.

Club History

With numerous La Liga, Copa del Rey, and UEFA Champions League titles, FC Barcelona has a storied legacy. The club has also been home to some of the greatest players in football history, including Lionel Messi, Johan Cruyff, and Andrés Iniesta.
